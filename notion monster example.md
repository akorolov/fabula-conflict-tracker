A large centipede that can roll itself into a ball to fend off attacks, only to spring up and bite afterward.

## Basic Attacks

**Mandible Slash** • 【DEX + MIG】 • 【HR + 5】 **poison** damage and the target suffers **weak**.

**Cutter Ball** • 【DEX + MIG】 • 【HR + 5】 **physical** damage. If the Cutterpillar performed the Guard action during its previous turn, this attack deals 5 extra damage.

## Special Rules

**Roll Up** • When the Cutterpillar performs the **Guard** action, it gains Immunity to **physical** damage until the start of its next turn.
